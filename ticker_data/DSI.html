My Dock

Add modules

Conveniently access information that's important to you with this customizable dock.

Columns

The dock has four columns which can display a maximum of five modules per column. If you don't see all four columns, you can enlarge the width of your screen to see more of the dock. Green dots at the top of the dock indicate displayed columns. You can show a hidden column by selecting a gray dot.

Adding modules

Select More modules to see all of the available modules. Simply drag and drop the modules you want to display in the columns. Rearrange the modules within a column or between columns at any time to suit your needs.

How the modules work

You can customize dock modules to display the data you want by clicking on the gear icon to access the customizable features. Modules with help content have aicon for specific information about that module.

Show or hide the dock

You can show or hide the dock as needed. Clicknext to the My Dock header to collapse the dock area. When the dock is collapsed, clickat the top of the dock toolbar on the far right to expand the dock for display.

Dock feature updates

Look periodically for updates to the dock that will give you even more choices and flexibility.
