Company profile

Ameresco, Inc. provides clean technology and renewable energy supply solutions. The Company offers enterprise energy management services, consulting services, and the sale of solar PV energy products and systems. The Company’s segments include U.S. Regions, U.S. Federal, Canada, and Non-Solar Distributed Generation (Non-Solar DG). The U.S. Regions, U.S. Federal, and Canada segments offer energy efficiency products and services, which include the design, engineering, and installation of equipment and other measures. It also offers renewable energy solutions and services, which include the construction of small-scale plants. The Non-Solar DG segment sells electricity, thermal, processed renewable gas fuel, or biomethane produced from renewable sources of energy, other than solar, and generated by small-scale plants. The Company’s services include the development, design, arrangement of financing, construction, and installation services.

ResearchTeamTM is a unique third-party research tool that employs a 'wisdom of crowds' approach to investment research. The concept is simple: a combination (a team) of different research providers providing company recommendations perform better than the recommendations of individual research providers alone. Performance measurement is traditionally used to find the "best" research provider. Certain providers may have poor performance, but may still add overall value if their strengths are used in combination with another research provider.

ResearchTeamTM research is a team-based approach to investment research, based on the universe of companies covered by four underlying third-party research providers-CFRA, Ford Equity Research, TheStreet and MarketEdge. Rules are applied to the four providers' recommendations to form an overall "team recommendation" for each company covered. Following the "wisdom of crowds" idea, the theory is that the team recommendation will outperform the individual provider recommendations.

ResearchTeamTM reports provide you with a complete rating picture including the current ResearchTeamTM rating, each independent provider's rating, and the historical accuracy and performance of ResearchTeamTM ratings.

Market Edge Rating Overview

Every security in the Market Edge universe carries a Long, Neutral from Long, Neutral from Avoid or Avoid opinion. Market Edge bases each security opinion on seven, non-correlated, technical indicators that carry different weightings. These indicators include relative strength, volume, moving averages, and momentum studies.

  • A Long is a recommendation to buy.
  • An Avoid means the security is out of favor and not likely to rise in price.
  • A Neutral means no action is recommended on the security. Neutral ratings are listed as either Neutral from Long (a downgrade) or Neutral from Avoid (an upgrade).
Score

Market Edge's unique scoring system alerts you when the short-term technical condition of a security is improving or deteriorating. Scores range from -4 to +4.

  • Securities with Long ratings have scores ranging from 0 to -4. A -3 means the security is in trouble. A -4 represents the lowest possible score before Market Edge downgrades the security.
  • Securities with Avoid ratings have scores ranging from 0 to +4. A +3 means the security is turning around. A +4 represents the highest possible score before Market Edge upgrades the security.
